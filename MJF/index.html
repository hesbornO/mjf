Our Programs

Food security and social protection

MJF focuses on tackling food security by seeking to reduce hunger, achieve food security and improved nutrition and promote sustainable agriculture. In addition, it makes concerted efforts towards reducing poverty and vulnerability, building human capital, empowering women and girls, improving livelihoods, and responding to economic and other shocks

Water and health transformation

Sustainable management of water resources and access to safe water and sanitation are essential for unlocking economic growth and productivity, and provide significant leverage for existing investments in health and education

Basic and life long education

MJF seeks to promote and facilitate both basic and lifelong education to various individuals across the globe. It purposes to create creative, passionate lifelong learners who are not afraid to take risks. In addition, it imparts knowledge to its members which enables them to think critically when faced with various problems and be able to work in a collaborative environment.

Sustainable shelter and sanitation improvement

MJF is making concerted efforts to improve water quality by reducing pollution, eliminating dumping and minimizing release of hazardous chemicals and materials to the environment
